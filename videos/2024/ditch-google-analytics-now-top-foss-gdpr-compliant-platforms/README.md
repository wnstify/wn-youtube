In this video, we explore the vital topic of web analytics, focusing on the limitations of Google Analytics, particularly in terms of GDPR compliance and user privacy. We introduce and compare four leading open-source analytics platforms: Matomo, Plausible, Umami, and Ackee. These tools offer privacy-respecting, GDPR-compliant alternatives to Google Analytics, with capabilities for self-hosting on your servers. Real traffic data from our website is used to showcase how each of these platforms performs in practical scenarios, helping viewers make an informed decision on which analytics tool best suits their needs in the ever-evolving digital landscape of 2024.
